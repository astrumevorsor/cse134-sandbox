What is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu?

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) is a martial art and combat sport that focuses on grappling and ground fighting. It emphasizes technique and leverage over strength and size, making it an effective form of self-defense for people of all body types.
